Hopper Addon Docs

Installation

Install the addon from Blender Preferences: just choose 'Install', select the ZIP or folder, and enable it from the Add-ons tab under the "UV" category.

No special dependencies required. Compatible with the latest Blender versions.

Overview

Addon Panel Features

The main panel includes:
Setup Material – Instantly applies the atlas texture to your mesh.
Unwrap – Unwrap selected faces to the chosen tile.
Setup Viewport – Enable preview thumbnails for your atlas images.
Hotkeys – Fast access for common actions, all shown in the panel.

Workflow

Color palettes

Includes 4 pre-made, ready-to-use color palettes to streamline your texturing and boost creativity

Pick Tiles

Select and assign specific tiles from your atlas palette to your mesh for precise texturing control.

Randomize Tiles

Automatically assign random tiles from your atlas to create natural variation and avoid repetition in textures.

Scale

Scale UVs inside a tile for best fit using smooth interactive controls.

Move

Move UVs to fine-tune positions. Live preview allows drag-and-drop adjustments.

Rotate

Rotate UVs to align textures, with 90° snap and free rotation options.

Highlight & Shadow

Apply highlight or shadow effects on UVs for subtle lighting and texture variation.

Limitations

One object supports a single atlas texture only. Support for multiple atlases on the same object is in development and will be added in future versions.
